What is Tabeebi?

Tabeebi is a health directory that enables people in the GCC (Gulf) countries to find the nearest hospitals, pharmacies, clinics, and other medical institutions based on what their insurance networks cover.

How does it work

Once you download the app, select your medical insurance networks from the list. The app will request your permission to detect your city and area. Upon approval, you're all set! You can easily start searching through nearby medical outlets where you're covered.

Why use Tabeebi?

Tabeebi includes data from 22+ insurance networks covered and more than 6000 hospitals, clinics and pharmacies from the GCC countries: Bahrain, Kuwait, United Arab Emirates (UAE), Oman, Qatar - with the ability to search within their cities and streets.

How to Participate

Don't find your network or hospital/institution? Make us aware of it via the contact link below and we'll update our data accordingly, provided we could collect sufficient up-to-date information on said network or hospital.
