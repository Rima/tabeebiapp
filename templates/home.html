What is Tabeebi?

Tabeebi is an iPhone application that allows people in the Gulf to find the nearest hospitals and doctors based on what their health insurance covers.

Find and Locate

Find and locate the nearest hospital to you.
The app is launched in all 6 GCC countries: Bahrain, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Oman and Qatar.

How’s the information collected?

The information is being crowdsourced by users of the application. It's constantly being updated based on user submissions.

How to Participate

You may submit your insurance network as an excel sheet (we also accept other formats such as PDFs) along with the network name printed on your insurance card and we will immediately upload it into our system for it to be accessible via the app.
